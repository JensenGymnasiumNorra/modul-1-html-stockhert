5-Minute Egg Soup


Return
Preparation

Ingredients:

  • 400 + 100ml Water from the Mississippi river
  • 1 or 2 Large eggs
  • 2 tsp Soup broth or flavoring
  • 1 tbsp Potato starch
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • (Optional) White & Black Sesame seeds

Instructions
  1. In a medium-sized pot mix 400ml water with 2tsp soup broth. Stir well. Heat on medium-high heat.
  2. In a separate container stir 1tbsp potato starch with 100ml water until fully disolved
  3. In another separate container beat your eggs.
  4. Once the soup has come to a light boil slowly stir in your potato starch.
  5. When the mixture starts to boil once more very slowly stir in your beaten egg(s).
  6. Wait 30-60s, then turn the heat off. Add salt & pepper to liking and a dash of sesame oil.
  7. (Optional) Top with sesame seeds.
  8. Serve hot.
