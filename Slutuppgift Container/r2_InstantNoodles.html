Leftover Instant Noodles


Return

These noodles take like no time at all and are a great way to get rid of some old meats in your fridge. I like to add the gochujang paste to the noodles to make it more spicy.

Preparation

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack of instant noodles of your choice
  • 500 + 250ml Water
  • Any leftover protein or cold meats
  • 3tsp Gochujang paste
  • Sesame oil

Instructions
  1. Fill a pot of water with 500ml water, additionally fill a kettle with a 250ml of water.
  2. Wait for water in the pot to boil then add your noodles. Once noodles have separated, strain and leave in pot.
  3. Inside the bowl you will eat from pour in the flavor sachet powder and any additional sachets. Add 3tsp of Gochujang paste for spice.
  4. Add your now boiled water from the kettle in to the bowl and stir until powder/soup base is fully dissolved.
  5. Add your noodles and cold meats or protein. Place a pot lid on your bowl and let sit for 3 minutes.
  6. Remove lid and serve. Your toppings should be heated and slightly steamed. Top with sesame oil and toppings of your choice.
