Bread Sandwich

Let me introduce you to this absolute KILLER of a British delicacy. It's so delicious, I can't stop eating it. Now this, this is prime British cuisine.


Return
Preparation

Ingredients:

  • 3 Slices of bread
  • ~3tsp Butter

Instructions
  1. (Optional) Toast your slices of bread.
  2. Butter the slices. One of them should be buttered on both sides.
  3. Assemble your sandwich with the double sided slice in the middle.
  4. Reflect.
  5. Ponder.
  6. Serve.


Additional Information & Feedback
Ingredient Calories
3 slices of bread (≈ 25g each) ≈ 225 kcal
Butter (3 tsp ≈ 15g total) ≈ 108 kcal
